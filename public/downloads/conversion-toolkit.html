üé® The Above-the-Fold Formula

This formula has generated over $10M in revenue for our clients:

1. Compelling Headline (40% of conversion impact)

  • Address the main pain point or desire
  • Include a benefit or transformation
  • Keep under 10 words when possible
  • Example: "Double Your Website Conversions in 30 Days"

2. Supporting Subheadline (20% impact)

  • Expand on the main promise
  • Address skepticism or add credibility
  • Example: "Proven strategies used by 500+ successful businesses"

3. Visual Hero Element (25% impact)

  • Use faces when possible (increases trust 25%)
  • Show your product/service in action
  • Avoid generic stock photos

4. Primary Call-to-Action (15% impact)

  • Use action words: "Get", "Start", "Download", "Book"
  • Create urgency: "Start My Free Trial Today"
  • Make it visually prominent (contrasting colors)
