üìù Content Optimization Formula

The E-A-T Framework: Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
Google prioritizes content that demonstrates these three qualities.

Title Tag Optimization

  • Include primary keyword near the beginning
  • Keep under 60 characters
  • Make it compelling and click-worthy
  • Example: "SEO Guide 2024: Rank #1 on Google (Step-by-Step)"

Meta Description Magic

  • Summarize the page content in 150-160 characters
  • Include primary and secondary keywords naturally
  • Add a call-to-action when appropriate
  • Make it compelling to increase click-through rates

Header Structure (H1-H6)

  • One H1 tag per page with primary keyword
  • Use H2s for main sections
  • Use H3s for subsections
  • Maintain logical hierarchy
üí° Content Length Sweet Spot:
‚Ä¢ Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words
‚Ä¢ Service pages: 800-1,500 words
‚Ä¢ Product pages: 300-800 words
Quality always trumps quantity!
