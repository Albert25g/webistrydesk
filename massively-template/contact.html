Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost?

Our websites start from R5,000 for a professional business site. E-commerce stores start from R8,000. The final price depends on your specific requirements, features needed, and complexity. We provide detailed quotes with no hidden fees.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 1-2 weeks. E-commerce stores typically take 2-3 weeks. Custom web applications can take 3-6 weeks. We'll provide an accurate timeline with your quote based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All our websites include 3-6 months of free support (varies by package). After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans starting from R500/month for updates, security, and technical support.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive SEO and digital marketing services starting from R3,000/month. This includes local SEO, Google My Business optimization, social media marketing, and Google Ads management.

Do you work with businesses outside of Nelspruit?

Yes, we work with clients across South Africa and internationally. Most of our communication is done via WhatsApp, email, and video calls, making location irrelevant for most projects.

What makes WebistryDesk different?

We focus on results, not just pretty websites. Every website we build is designed to convert visitors into customers. We also provide personalized service - you'll work directly with experienced developers, not junior staff or account managers.
